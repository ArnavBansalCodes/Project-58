My Favorite Videogame...
Minecraft Dungeons!

My favorite game is Minecraft Dungeons for a number of reasons. First of all, it has characters and weapons based off another fun game named Minecraft . Minecraft Dungeons is a game where there is the Arch-Illager, and evil bad guy, who has taken over. It is your mission to complete quests, aquire armor and weapons, and destroy bad guys like witches, zombies, skeletons, spiders, and creepers. You can also collect items such as emeralds which can be sold for items, bows and arrows for long-range shooting, pets to help you in battle, enchantment points which can be used to upgrade your weapons and armor by giving them magical powers, and so much more!
